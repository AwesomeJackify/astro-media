What is Astro Media?

Astro Media was founded in early 2023 because we noticed the lack of quality websites in Auckland, New Zealand. We discovered that many small businesses were still using old websites that were slow and often had confusing layouts.

This led to Jack Gong, a Software Engineering student and Otis Wales, a Computer Science student to come together to make changes in this landscape.

Our primary mission is to deliver the best websites, that are Fully Custom , Beautiful and Affordable
